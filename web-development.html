FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What kinds of companies?

We work with all kinds of companies ranging from oil, construction, contractors, plumbers, real-estate agents, nutraceuticals, medical offices, and much more. Our team of highly skilled web developers, designers, marketers, and advertisers are trained to work in a wide variety of areas to help grow your business.

What are typical considerations?

When considering our quoting process the number of pages, complexity of the project, and time needed to complete the project are our main factors in determining the price of the project. As a customer you should always ensure you don't get over charged and also have full access to the site after completion so no matter what happens you'll never have to go buy a new site.

Should I expect to pay fees?

We ensure that the final price of the project is stated and agreed upon before we even begin the project. We do allow for additional changes to be made throughout the life of the project, but any costs from that will be stated up-front before you decide to make the changes. We work with full transparency on our projects so both parties are happy with the outcome and price.

How much does it cost after the year of free hosting is over?

After the first year of free hosting is done the pricing can vary from $0 per year to around $500 per year depending on your hosting needs. We will be 100% transparent with the cost before hand of the hosting required for your site so you don't have any surprises later down the road.
