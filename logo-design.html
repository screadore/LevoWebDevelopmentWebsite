Frequently Asked Questions

What kind of logo's can you make?

We can make logo's of all kinds. We work with Law firms, Medical offices, Construction companies, Oil companies, Contractors, Real Estate Agents, and so on. Our team of marketers and designers are highly skilled at what they do to bring you the highest quality logo that will help your brand stand out over your competitors.

How long does it typically take to get a completed logo?

It typically takes 1-5 days for completion of a logo with all revision included. If you order the trademark package it typically takes a few months for the trademark and patent office to get back to you, but your logo will be done way before the final decision of your trademark.

Should I expect to pay any additional fees?

Nope. No additional fees are required unless you decided to upgrade your package during the process. All fees are included.
