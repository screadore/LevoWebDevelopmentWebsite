Frequently Asked Questions

What kinds of Ad campaigns?

We make ad campaigns specific to your target market and business needs. We can generate leads, increase sales, and grow your social media. We track key metrics to enhance your success rate and lower your pay-per-click cost across all platforms.

What are some typical considerations?

We consider the target audience you want to reach or develop a new target audience if you don't have one in mind already. Then we consider their geographic locations and interests to then target specific individuals who have a higher chance of buying or using your service/product.

Should I expect to pay fees?

The only additional fees would be paying the advertising costs. They are not included in the cost of our service, but we will work with you to find the perfect budget and ensure the advertisements don't go over that budget.

How do you grow our social media following?

We have state of the art social media growth programs that help your company grow, while posting engaging content to get people interested in your brand while also bringing them back to see more.
