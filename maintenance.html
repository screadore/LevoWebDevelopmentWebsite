FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Why should I do this?

Website security is a big deal to your customers and it should be to you as well. We will make sure your site is secure from hackers. Another thing to look out for is when you update a plugin it may not work correctly so we can check it and ensure everything is working perfectly on your site.

Why is updating my website important?

Updating your site monthly will keep customers coming back to your site to explore new content, along with providing more information for Google to index to improve the ranking of your website so you get seen more in search engines.

Why are database backups for my website important?

It's important to backup your website just incase the unthinkable happens and your site information is lost. This will keep your customer information in a safe and secure place so you can avoid any setbacks. This is especially important for ecommerce websites as they have tons of client information.
