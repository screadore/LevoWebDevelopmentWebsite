Web Developer

As a web developer you are responsible for breathing life into our product.

Requirements and Responsibilities

  • Enjoy interesting challenges and solving problems.
  • Love to work with a well diversed team.
  • Exceptional design portfolio of high quality work.
  • Proficient knowledge of Photoshop, Illustrator and InDesign.
  • Experience with Webdesign (HTML, CSS, Java, JQuery, PHP) and basic knowledge of Motion Design.
  • Ability to design interaction models for mobile and web solution.

  • Well-versed in web technologies, best practices and emerging trends.
  • Motivated to manage your own projects and work under tight deadlines.
